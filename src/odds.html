IAP Pack Ticket Odds

IAP scratch-off packs contain tickets drawn from the same card tiers listed above. Each pack's specific ticket composition is disclosed at the time of purchase in the ScratchShop. All IAP tickets are subject to the same per-tier odds as free daily cards, with Legend Bundle boost applied when applicable.

Guaranteed Prizes

All guaranteed prizes listed in a pack's description are awarded at the time of purchase and are not subject to random draw. The odds tables above apply only to the outcome of individual scratch-off ticket reveals (cash amounts and symbol matching), not to the delivery of guaranteed pack prizes.
